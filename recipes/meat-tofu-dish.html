Meat and Tofu Dish (肉豆腐)

Description

I found this dish at a Taiwanese recipe website. All credit goes to 東京的陳太太. It was advertised as a dish that is quick and easy to make.

This recipe is for two people.

Ingredients

  • Tofu, pick 木棉
  • Ground pork and beef, 100g
  • Green onion, as needed

Sauce

  • Soy sauce, 3 tablespoon
  • Mirin, 3 tablespoon
  • Brown sugar, 1 tablespoon

Thickening Agent

  • Water, 100g
  • Corn starch, moderate amount

Steps

  1. Cut the tofu into equal pieces. Make the sauce.
  2. Stir fry the ground meat until the color turns brown.
  3. Add in the tofu and pre-made sauce. Set low heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Make the thickening agent. Turn tofu around.
  5. Add in the thickening agent before taking the food out of the pan.
  6. Add some diced green onion on top. Complete.
