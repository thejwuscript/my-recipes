Mentaiko Udon

Description

This is an easy-to-make late night comfort food. For beginners but taste good.

I found this receipe on Facebook. Please follow along.

Ingredients

  • Udon, 1 pack
  • Onion, one-quarter
  • Garlic as needed for flavour
  • Pork, bacon, or any sliced meat
  • Condensed milk/whipped cream
  • Black pepper
  • Dashi (salt)
  • Mentaiko
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Chop the quarter-sized onion.
  2. Set medium heat. Add a bit of oil into the frying pan. Add in the chopped up onion pieces.
  3. Add in meat slices. Stir fry a bit.
  4. Add udon and condensed milk into the pan. Stir.
  5. Add in black pepper and small amount of dashi.
  6. Stir some more.
  7. Turn off the heat. Serve it on a plate.
  8. Add seaweed and mentaiko.
  9. Done!
