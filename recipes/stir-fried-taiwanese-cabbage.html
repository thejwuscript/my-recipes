Stir-fried Taiwanese Cabbage

image via here

Description

Because I missed eating this dish from Taiwanese restaurants in Vancouver, I began searching for a recipe online.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage, one-quarter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Sprinkle some salt on the cabbage.
  2. Heat the pan. It has to be very hot.
  3. Add in oil and then garlic.
  4. Add cabbage. Stir fry a bit.
  5. Add a bit of water. Cover the pan for 30 seconds.
  6. Stir some more. Add some black pepper. Complete.
